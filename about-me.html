About Me

My name is Todd Croak-Falen, and I've designed and coded mobile-first web pages for Acura, BMW, Chrysler, Chevrolet, Dodge, Harley-Davidson, Honda, Hyundai, Jeep, Kia, Nissan, Ram, and Toyota dealerships nationwide.

I particularly love finding creative ways to modify the graphic design of a page for different screen sizes using HTML5/CSS3. With each new project, I push my skill set a little deeper by trying new techniques and further embedding the ones I already have.

My other interests include creative writing, reading fiction and nonfiction, electronic music, hiking, cycling, personal finance, investing, chess, sensory deprivation tanks, and Krav Maga.

I am based in West Los Angeles, CA.
