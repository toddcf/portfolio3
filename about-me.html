About Me

My name is Todd Croak-Falen, and I am currently a Front End Developer and MarTech programmer at Princess Cruises. My main focus is migrating and refactoring all tracking pixels from Adobe DTM to Launch. I also debug existing pixels and use AppDynamics and ObservePoint to monitor the site for issues.

Previously, I designed and coded mobile-first web pages for Acura, BMW, Chrysler, Chevrolet, Dodge, Harley-Davidson, Honda, Hyundai, Jeep, Kia, Nissan, Ram, and Toyota dealerships nationwide at fusionZONE Automotive.

I also coded the user interface for an iPad kiosk in The North Face retail stores that allows customers to select and play different videos on a big screen TV.

My passion for coding is twofold: I love the intense satisfaction of planning and executing the logic of JavaScript, and I also love finding creative ways to use HTML5 and CSS3 to reflow the graphic design of a page on different screen sizes. With each new project, I push my skill set a little deeper by trying new techniques and further embedding the ones I already have.

My other interests include creative writing, reading fiction and nonfiction, electronic music, hiking, cycling, personal finance, investing, chess, sensory deprivation tanks, and Krav Maga.

I am based in West Los Angeles, CA.
